Egusi Soup

Description

Egusi Soup is a meal made from melon seed. It is popular amongst Nigerian. It can be paired with swallows such as pounded yam, fufu, semovita etc. Egusi soup is loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Egusi/melon seed
  • Palm Oil
  • Crayfish
  • Pepper
  • Stockfish
  • Meat
  • Vegetable

Steps

  • Start by boiling the proteins i.e meat and stockfish. Season to taste
  • After they are well cooked, keep aside. In a new pot, heat the palm oil
  • Fry the grounded melon seed. Make sure to stir to prevent burn
  • Add the stock water and proteins
  • Add vegetables and turn off the heat shortly after
