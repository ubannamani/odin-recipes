Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is a popular delicacy in West Africa. It originate from Senegal but has been widely adopted by many West African countries especially Ghana and Nigeria. There is an ongoing debate as to which country makes the best jollof rice. My answer is Nigeria, of course (mostly because I am Nigerian lol)

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Spices
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Onions
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Start by parboiling the rice
  2. Wash the parboiled rice and keep aside
  3. In a clean pot, fry the blended pepper and tomatoes mix thoroughly
  4. Add spices to give flavor to the meal
  5. Add just enough water to cook the rice
  6. Pour in the parboiled rice
  7. Allow to cook for 40-60 minutes, depending on the quantity of the rice
  8. Serve with any protein of your choice.
