Chicken and Chips

Description

Chicken and chips is a fast meal, easy to make and very delicious. It is popular worldwide. The chips are usually made from potatoes. This meal is available in many fast food outlet worldwide

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Season the chicken thoroughly and marinate for 1-3 hours
  2. Air fry or bake chicken till tender
  3. Next, slice your potatoes into straight rod-like shapes
  4. Deep fry the potato chips in hot oil or airfry
  5. Serve in a platter with a side of ketchup
